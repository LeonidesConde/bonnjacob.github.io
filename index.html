Welcome

"The ‘Pig n Whistle’ is a modern British style pub situated in Agujo,Daanbantayan,Cebu. It serves the surrounding area including Cebu Technological University and it’s not just for the them, coz you get loads of locals and Students across Daanbantayan here, so it’s probably because it looks so inviting. Outside there’s a lovely terrace, all wooden tables and benches, inside it’s big and spacious, with comfortable seating, an awesome menu, loads of blackboards and signs, 9 big TV screens showing all the live sports, often 4 or 5 games at the same time on Saturday, including the NBA games and the atmosphere is absolutely awesome!"
